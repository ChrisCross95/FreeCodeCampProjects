Shanetta Cross

Dedicated Professional. Devoted Mother. Loyal Friend

Pictured here Radiating Energy

My mother is the strongest and most authentic person I know
She was born in Milkwaukee, Wisconsin, though she, like most of
our family, have strong roots in central Mississipi. As one of six
children (and the youngest of three sisters), my mother developed
sharp people skills eary on: she is as intuitive and discerning in
her analysis of indivuduals and social dynamics as the most seasoned
sociologist or clinical psychologist. My mother has always been
honest in her opinion, true to her word, and relentless in her
advocacy for lifelong education and personal development.

My mother became pregnant with me at the tender age of fifteen,
and gave birth to me the same year: 1995. During this period and
all that followed, she embodied the pride, work ethic, and
entrepreneurialisme of our family, which has included
public servants, machanists, and even moonshiners! Following paths
beaten by millions of African Americans before her, she ventured
north, to the industrial city of Fort Wayne, Indiana, where she
worked tirelessly, thanklessly, and with little support to provide
me with a firm and stable foundation from which to grow and explore.
She left behind the familiarity of childhood settings and welcoming
communiites to face the stings of racism and exclusion in a distant
city.
